2

2021

After I gained a solid foundation of knowledge and skills in cybersecurity, I became interested in exploring the world of bug hunting and web vulnerability.
To do this, I joined online platforms such as Bugcrowd and Intigrity, which provide opportunities for aspiring bug hunters to test their skills and earn rewards for identifying and reporting vulnerabilities.

Through these platforms, I had the opportunity to learn about different types of web vulnerabilities and how to exploit them.
I also learned about the importance of responsible disclosure and the ethical considerations involved in bug hunting.

In addition to participating in bug bounty programs, I also started creating my own web applications in order to learn more about the development process and how to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities. This involved a lot of hard work and dedication,
but it was incredibly rewarding and helped me to gain a deeper understanding of web vulnerability and how to protect against it.

Overall, my experiences with bug hunting and web vulnerability
have been incredibly valuable in helping me to learn new things and develop my skills in the field of cybersecurity. I am excited to continue exploring and learning in this exciting and dynamic field.
